Macros available

Legend:
  S/D - Static (S) or Dynamic (D) macro. Dynamic macros are slow.
  BL  - battle loading screen
  PP  - players panel
  SF  - statistic form (Tab)
  MM  - minimap
  VM  - vehicle markers
  HI  - hitlog
  HL  - hpLeft

  'X' - available
  ' ' - not available (can return wrong value - '', 'NaN', 'undefined', ...)
  '.' - TODO
  '-' - Obsoleted (will be replaced with other macro)
